Ultra chocolate milk

Thirsty for some thick chocolate milk? LOOK NO FURTER!!! Here's a tried and true perfect recipe for the best chocolate milk in history!

Ingredients:

  • Any milk
  • Chocolate milk powder, preferably Oboy
  1. Pour a glass of milk just short of full
  2. Take 5 spoon-fulls of chocolate milk powder and pour it into the milk
  3. Mix with the spoon
  4. Enjoy
