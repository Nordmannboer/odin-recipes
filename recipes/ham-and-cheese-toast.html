Ham and cheese toast

Main page

You're hungry and want a fast, delicious meal? well no worries! This simple recipe for a ham and cheese toast is perfect.

Ingredients:

  • White bread
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • butter
  1. Toast the bread in a toaster
  2. Apply butter on the bread with a knife while the bread is still hot
  3. Put on a ton of ham and cheese to your liking
  4. Eat the delicious ham and cheese toast
