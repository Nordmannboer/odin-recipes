Pepperoni and pineapple pizza

This pizza is one of my favorite dishes! It's really simple to make and won't take long, but it's still delicious!

Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pineapple slices
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Oregano
  1. Mix the flour, yeast, olive oil, and salt together in a bowl, until it becomes a dough
  2. If the dough is sticky, add some more flour and mix it until it has a nice texture
  3. Let the dough grow in the bowl under a sheet of plastic for 30 minutes
  4. Turn on your oven to 225 degrees Celsius, while shaping the dough into a pizza shape
  5. Smear the tomato sauce across the pizza dough, and afterwards spread the pepperoni slices and pineapple evenly across the surface
  6. Finally pour the cheese all over the pizza, and put the pizza into the heated oven for about 15 minutes
  7. Take the pizza out of the oven, and let it cool down for 5 minutes and voila, you're in for a treat
